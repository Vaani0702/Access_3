About Our Platform

Careers Without Limits is dedicated to creating an inclusive job market for people with physical disabilities. Through AI-driven personalized training, we ensure that every individual receives the skills they need to succeed. Our mission is to break down barriers and empower disabled professionals with job-ready expertise.

How We Use Web3 & NFTs

We leverage blockchain technology to issue NFT-backed certificates, ensuring authenticity and permanence. These digital certificates are stored on the Solana blockchain, making them tamper-proof and universally verifiable.

By integrating Web3, users can securely own their achievements and share them with employers worldwide. Employers can instantly verify credentials without the need for intermediaries, increasing trust in the hiring process.

Why We Made This Project

We believe that everyone, regardless of physical ability, deserves a chance to build a fulfilling career. This project was created to bridge the employment gap, empower disabled individuals with the right tools, and ensure equal opportunities in the digital workforce.
